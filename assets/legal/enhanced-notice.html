Enhanced Notice

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Enhanced Notice provides additional transparency about how Selection handles data for users in regions that require enhanced privacy disclosures.

Categories of personal information

  • Identifiers (email, account ID)
  • Location data (postal code you enter)
  • Commercial information (saved deals and lists)
  • Internet or app activity (in-app interactions)

Purposes of processing

  • Provide and personalize the flyer browsing experience.
  • Maintain shopping lists and favorites.
  • Improve app reliability and customer support.
  • Send optional promotional notifications with your consent.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain processing via Privacy Choices in the app.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

Do not sell or share

Selection does not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

How to submit a request

Use Settings → Privacy Choices in the app, or email privacy@selection.app with the subject line “Privacy Request”.

Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy, unless a longer period is required by law.
