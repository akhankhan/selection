Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MENU2GO collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — email, display name, and profile photo if you sign in.
  • Location — postal code you provide to show relevant flyers (stored on your device and optionally synced with your account).
  • Usage data — shopping lists, favorites, and search history stored locally or in your account.
  • Device data — basic diagnostics to improve app performance and fix issues.

2. How we use information

  • Display flyers and deals relevant to your area.
  • Save your lists, favorites, and preferences.
  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Respond to support requests and store submissions.
  • Send push notifications if you opt in.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers (such as Firebase/Google Cloud) that help us operate the app, subject to appropriate safeguards.

4. Your choices

  • Clear search history and shopping lists in Settings.
  • Manage notification preferences in Settings.
  • Review privacy choices in the Privacy Choices screen.
  • Delete your account by contacting support.

5. Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law. Local data on your device persists until you clear it or uninstall the app.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections and Firebase security rules. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children

MENU2GO is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@selection.app.
